[CrossDevicePrefTracker] Introduce CrossDevicePrefTrackerFactory

This change introduces CrossDevicePrefTrackerFactory, a
ProfileKeyedServiceFactory responsible for creating and managing
CrossDevicePrefTracker instances for chrome/browser.

This is the first step in making the service available to clients.
Future CLs will expose CrossDevicePrefTracker to Chrome on Android and
Chrome on iOS.
