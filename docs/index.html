[PA] Reduce number of casts throughout PA

There is plenty of casting between void*, char*, uintptr_t. Most of the
time, PA operates on addresses and does a lot of arithmetic on them,
making uintptr_t more appropriate.

This change is expected to be a no-op.

This is just the beginning, there are many other places in need of
conversion.
