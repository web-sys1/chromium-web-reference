Update MediaApp sort ordering from Z-first to A-first.

We originally chose z-first so that the app emulated a "swipe right to
go to previously taken image" UX, mirroring a camera roll. However,
from numerous feedback reports we've learnt that this is confusing.
Especially given that users often sort by name and many photos and
song titles encode an implied lexicographic order e.g.
"PHOTO_DCIM_00001.jpeg", "01 - Song Title by Someone".

This issue is extremely obvious for the audio play queue view, where
we have a track number that conflicts with our current reverse
lexicographic ordering.

This CL instead updates the ordering to A-first to fix and aide the
aforementioned issues.
