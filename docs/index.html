Check Drive Quota and local volume space before copy or move in

When we call statvfs() on drivefs, it always returns to us that it has 1
exobyte of space available, so we currently always allow a copy or move
operation into Drive. This can be a problem when either we don't have
enough local disk, or we are out of Drive quota.

We now check both.
