TabsFromOtherDevices side panel: Contents

This populates the actual html/ts contents for the TabsFromOtherDevices
side panel. Specifically, there's a device picker at the top, and then
a list of tabs for that device below, with title, URL, and timestamp.

This also required a minor change to ForeignSessionHandler and the
corresponding mojom, to add the per-device timestamp string.
(Previously, there was only a raw timestamp per tab, and a timestamp
string only per device.)

Here's what it looks like: https://screenshot.googleplex.com/UAELQ2Hw5Rzzhdo
