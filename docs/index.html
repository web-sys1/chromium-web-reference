dpwas: Adjust WebAppFrameToolbarView height and y-pos

The inconsistency in height & y-pos of WebAppFrameToolbarView (WAFTV) in
standalone vs WCO mode causes the icons to shift by ~1px quite visibly
when the toggle button is pressed repeatedly.

This CL ensures that the height & y-pos of the WAFTV is
consistent in standalone mode and in WCO mode by having the height match
the caption buttons container.
