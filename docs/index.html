[browser] Add BrowserWindowInterface::RegisterBrowserDidClose

This CL extends BrowserWindowInterface to allow clients to listen
for the the success of a browser close request.

This is used in downstream CLs to ensure BrowserWindowInterface
pointers held by WebUIBrowserWindowContext are safely invalidated
in the case embedders fail to invalidate explicitly.
