[Structured metrics] Check consent before saving platform2 metrics

When recording metrics from platform2 ('external metrics'), two consent
checks happen:

1. Before writing the metrics to disk in platform2.
2. Before upload in chromium.

We are intending to remove check 1 for RMA metrics because it cannot be
verified at the time of upload. To compensate, this CL adds a third
check when metrics are read from disk in chromium.
