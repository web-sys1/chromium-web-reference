Move EmptyEmbedderDelegate to GlicInstanceImpl

This change moves the ownership of the EmptyEmbedderDelegate from the
GlicInactiveSidePanelUi to the GlicInstanceImpl. This is to prevent a
use-after-free bug where the GlicInactiveSidePanelUi and its delegate
could be destroyed while the host still holds a pointer to the
delegate.
