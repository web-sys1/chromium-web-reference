[RemoveLegacy] Remove `BidiResolver` and its related classes

This patch removes the legacy `BidiResolver` and its related
classes.

This includes switching the rest of callers to NG:
* `ListedElement::FindCustomValidationMessageTextDirection`
* `HTMLElement`, `ResolveAutoDirectionality` and
  `AdjustDirectionalityIfNeededAfterChildrenChanged`.
* A variation of `ConstructTextRun`, used by
  `DateTimeFieldElement`, file upload control, menu list, and
  `layout_text_control`.
