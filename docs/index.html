Remove unused obsolete download stuff in "IME-Service <--> .so" API.

These are meant for CrOS 1P IME shared library ".so", hosted in the
IME service container, to call back into the IME service container.
However, these are no longer called anywhere in the ".so", and that's
been the case for quite a long time.

Virtual functions are tweaked to "void Unused...()", instead of
outright removals, to retain ABI vtable compatibility with the ".so"
that #include's the same header interfaces.h. We unfortunately can
never instantly adapt the ".so" at the same time in the current setup.
The two sides are cross-repo with one-way copybara from here to ".so".
