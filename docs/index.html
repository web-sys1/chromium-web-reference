WebUI: Standardize font{size,family} replacements/loadTimeData entries.

Normally C++ i18n replacements are derived from loadTimeData entries,
such that the names always match across the two data structures.

Previously 'fontSize', 'fontFamily', 'textDirection' was used in
GetWebUiCssTextDefaults(), whereas 'fontsize', 'fontfamily',
'textdirection' was used in webui::SetLoadTimeDataDefaults(), which
was confusing, and was also forcing shared_resources_data_source.cc to
manually register such replacements, as opposed to relying on the
default replacements that are registered for any WebUIDataSource.
