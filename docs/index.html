Original change's description:
> Add support for batching fetch processing in blink
>
> This pauses the ResourceLoadScheduler to accumulate fetch requests
> while the HTML parser is processing a chunk of HTML (or tokens from
> a background tokenizer).
>
> Instead of operating on each fetch individually as they come through,
> this allows the scheduler to make prioritization decisions on all of
> the fetches that were discovered/generated at a time.
>
> In the short term, it will increase the likelihood that
> higher-priority resources are not delayed by lower-priority resources
> that were discovered sooner.
>
> This is focused on the batching support and adding batching specifically to the fetches initiated by resources parsed from the HTML.
>
> In the longer-term, it lays the foundation for batching the requests
> through Mojo to the Network service and for batching fetches initiated by script.
>
