Reset NavigationRequests that use a speculative RFH being discarded

A NavigationRequest that already has a RenderFrameHost set (after it
received its final response) but isn't owned by the RenderFrameHost yet
(e.g. if it's deferred at WillProcessResponse) needs to be deleted on
or before the speculative RFH's destruction, to avoid having a
NavigationRequest that points to an already-deleted RenderFrameHost.
