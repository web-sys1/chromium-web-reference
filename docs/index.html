[String matching] Fix edit distance transposition cost.

Within the DP table, the cost of a transposition operation should always
be one.

Note that this CL doesn't technically change the behavior of the
algorithm, but simply unobfuscates a strange case. i.e.:

Old version:

When the two suffix characters of first_string[:i] and
second_string[:j] are the same (i.e. "...aa" and "...aa"), consider a
transposition with cost zero.

New version:

Do not consider zero-cost transpositions.

Pseudocode example:
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Damerau%E2%80%93Levenshtein_distance#Optimal_string_alignment_distance
