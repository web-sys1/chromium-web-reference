[SmartLock] Backoff BLE scanning after timeout

This change is to stop bluetooth scanning for 1 second before resuming
after an initial scan timeout, dropped connection, or authentication
failure. This change is hidden behind the
kSmartLockBluetoothScanningBackoff flag so the improvement on stability
on the lock screen can be measured in an experiment.

TEST=Added unittests to cover new functionality at 100% and 97% coverage
inside ProximityAuthRemoteDeviceLifeCycleImplTest and
ProximityAuthUnlockManagerImplTest respectively. Manually deployed and
tested on atlas device by producing an initial scanning timeout before a
GATT connection can be established and used logs to observe the
scanning backoff and successfully restart.
