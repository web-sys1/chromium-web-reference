Multiplanar - Remove GLCommonFactory dependency from IOSurfaceBacking

GL formats and makes complicated to untangle them. This has also crept
into many backings and would be nice to clean up. This change removes
this dependency from IOSurfaceBackingFactory and localizes the usages
needed within the factory.
