Revert "Merge ContextLifecycleNotifier into MojoBindingContext."

This reverts commit 03ad8ab3ca36d5574cf107251d0f47a867288975.

Reason for revert: After some more discussion and thought, we have reached the conclusion that many users of ContextLifecycleNotifier/Observer don't care about the Mojo capabilities introduced by MojoBindingContext. As such, this CL reverts the merging of the 2 classes, with future CLs making MojoBindingContext extend ContextLifecycleNotifier.
