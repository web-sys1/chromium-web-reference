Reorganize the machine_type for android emulator builders

For 4-cores, we have 400 n1 and 340 e2 bots. We used to differentiate
the machine_type between n1 and e2. But this bring unnecessary
monitoring effort for bot usage when adding more test suites to
emulator builders.

With this CL:
* For 4-cores, we use "or" dimension to point to either of n1 or e2.
* For 8-cores, since there are only 80 n1 (vs 1000+ e2), let's just
  use e2. Meanwhile I will convert the n1 into e2.
