Offset Android gesture data to account for onscreen position of app

When using stylus gestures, Blink tries to apply them in the wrong place. This is happening because the coordinates we get from Android are in the screen coordinate space (from the top left of the screen) but Blink needs coordinates from the top left of the web contents.

Previously, we offset the coordinates for the browser controls (omnibox etc.) but didn't take into account the Android status bar or whether the app was in split screen. This CL will mean that both are considered.
