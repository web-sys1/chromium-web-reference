smartsorting: promote apps onto screen in non-fixmore mode

This change causes smartsorting to promote apps onto the screen,
even when the ranking is larger than the screen size. Specifically,
this change reintroduces the concept of the "fold", which is how
many app slots there are on the screen, and has the promotion logic
target one of the first fold slots instead of the first length slots.
