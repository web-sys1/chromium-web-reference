ui/display: Retry logic to consider refresh rates

This CL updates the display configurator's retry logic to consider
refresh rates when downgrading a display's resolution upon modeset failure. We do so by first looking at a display's resolution, and
if they match, compare their refresh rates.

For a detailed list of product design requirements, see:
ChromeOS Monitor Behavior
