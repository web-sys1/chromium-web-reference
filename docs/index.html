[iOS] Fix BestFeaturesScreenCoordinator delegate forwarding

BestFeaturesScreenCoordinator previously forwarded its
FirstRunScreenDelegate directly to its detail screen coordinator. When
the detail screen completed, it passed itself to the delegate, failing
CHECK_EQ in FirstRunCoordinator.

This change makes BestFeaturesScreenCoordinator act as an intermediate
FirstRunScreenDelegate for the detail screen coordinator.
