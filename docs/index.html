Introduce MojoBindingContext in blink/public/platform.

MojoBindingContext encapsulates the necessary information for binding
Mojo interfaces, enabling parts of platform/ that provide them to
associate the interfaces with the correct context(e.g.,
ExecutionContext).

The CL also uses the MojoBindingContext to convert the FileUtilitiesHost
interface to be associated with an ExecutionContext.
