[NTP Customization] Fix clicking issue of the bottom sheets

Before this CL, clicking blank space on the bottom sheet while enabling
the talk back will invoke one of the click listener

As shown in the video, after clicking the empty space, the click
listener of the 2nd list item appears to be invoked.
https://drive.google.com/file/d/19X3cjbkSDhBBLrgmAX6zO_hir0MRTNmi/view?resourcekey=0-KdwSG4CP8UqiRFG2tIkP_

After this change, clicking whitespace on the bottom sheets no longer
incorrectly triggers list item clicks when TalkBack is enabled. Direct
clicks on list items function as expected with and without TalkBack.
Whitespace clicks without TalkBack remain non-interactive, as intended.
https://drive.google.com/file/d/1I3dT-QlOg4ILXiBWaCK-kS-qFjXdqIBO/view?usp=sharing&resourcekey=0-Os4TBmwYTkMvN5G6pJtqfw
