Fix StatusAreaWidget View ownership

This Widget held on to std::unique_ptr<View>, making them effectively
set_owned_by_client(). This relied on ~StatusAreaWidget happening before
~Widget would have deleted them from the View hierarchy as `delete View`
removes the View from the hierarchy (does not double-delete).

Owning Views that are inside the View hierarchy is being disallowed and
set_owned_by_client() is being removed. This changes the ownership model
so that StatusAreaWidget holds on to pointers owned by the View
hierarchy and the View hierarchy gets cleared out in ~Widget instead of
implicitly through ~unique_ptr in ~StatusAreaWidget.

Destruction still happens roughly at the same point in time but there
are no unique_ptrs to Views that are really owned by the hierarchy, or
Views owned by the hierarchy that are really owned outside of it.
