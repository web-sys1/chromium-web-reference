Implement support for SEI recovery in VTVideoDecodeAccelerator.

Long ago our H264 parser didn't support SEI recovery points and
there was concern that unverified SEI points may lead to leaking
uninitialized memory from the system. Our parser now has support
and the uninitialized memory concerns are just as relevant with a
standard IDR. As such implement support for SEI recovery now.
