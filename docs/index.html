emoji-picker: implement text-labeled subcategory bar for emoticons.

* render emoji-based or text-based subcategory bar conditionally,
depending on the selected category to match Figma mock (text group
button for emoticons, icon group button for emoji).

* hide chevrons and small blue indicator for text-labelled
subcategory bar temporarily.

* add new subcategory data for emoticons. It is used for rendering group
tabs.

Behavior note:
* Click at emoticon category button, the subcategory bar with TEXT
buttons is displayed.
* Click at emoji category button, the subcategory bar with EMOJI buttons
is displayed.
* Click at any tab at emoticon tab, it is expected there is neither any
update nor scrolling behavior.
