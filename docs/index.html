android: Extend UMA expiration date for RELRO creation success rate

The histogram ChromiumAndroidLinker.RelroProvidedSuccessfully is one of
the two remaining gistograms (along with
ChromiumAndroidLinker.RelroSharingStatus2) tracking health of the RELRO
sharing feature.

Currently the error rate on RelroProvidedSuccessfully is very close to
0%.

I suggest to extend expiration for one more year. Keeping this metric is
not super critical, rather a first thing to check when the primary
histogram is showing problems.
