[Extensions] Rename ExtensionPrefs::[G|S]etActivePermissions()

The "active permissions" stored in ExtensionPrefs do not necessarily
reflect the permissions active on the extension object, but rather the
permissions the extension desires to be active. These may be
substantially different due to permissions that may be withheld by the
user or revoked by enterprise policy.

Rename the methods on ExtensionPrefs to be GetDesiredActivePermissions()
and SetDesiredActivePermissions() to better convey this to callers.

This CL has no behavior change.
