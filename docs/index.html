[iOS] Adding OptimizationGuide Flag to chrome://flags

Previously, users were not being redirected to a product's webpage when
tapping on a price tracking push notification. After investigation, it
was discovered that there was an OptimizationGuide flag that controlled
whether the OptimizationGuide's push notification client was added to
the PushNotificationClientManager effectively controlling whether or not
tapping on a push notification for price tracking was supported.

In this CL, the OptimizationGuide's flag is added to chrome://flags to
allow users to enable it for their devices.
