Original change's description:
> Omit frame pointers in Skia's Raster Pipeline
>
> Skia's RP performs CPU shading with a series of tail-called
> "stages". Each stage is relatively short - often just a few
> instructions. In this system, the frame-pointer overhead is
> significant. We've shown this in Skia benchmarks, and would
> like to measure the impact on Chrome's rendering speed.
>
> We propose landing, then reverting this CL. Pinpoint will
> detect any regressions. Depending on the outcome of that,
> we can assess the value of landing this permanently.
