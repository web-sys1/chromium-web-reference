[ios] Ensure FeedTopSectionMediator unregister its observer

FeedTopSectionMediator creates and register an IdentityManager
observer. It is unregistered in its -shutdown method, but this
method was not called because -[FeedTopSectionCoordinator stop]
was not called on the -[NewTabPageCoordinator stop].

Calling -[FeedTopSectionCoordinator stop] should ensure that
the observer is properly unregistered.
