[anchor-position] Disallow custom properties in @position-fallback

I previously thought custom properties are good to have in
@position-fallback, but they are not straightforward to support as they
may be referenced by other properties not in @position-fallback, causing
dependency issues. Without a non-trivial spec, they cannot be supported.

Hence, this patch removes the support.
