Increase upper limits of ARC WM UMA histograms

Currently, in UMA dashboard, some arc wm histograms
(Arc.WM.WindowMaximizedDelayTime, Arc.WM.WindowClosedDelayTime,
Arc.WM.WindowEnterTabletModeDelayTime,
Arc.WM.WindowExitTabletModeDelayTime) are showing large-value data
beyond their maximum buckets. This CL increases the upper limits of
these histograms to better understand the distribution of these
large-value data points.
