[iOS] Add metrics for 3P intents in Incognito

Two enum histograms are added:
- `IOS.IncognitoInterstitial` to record actions within the Incognito
  Interstitial. By default, when the Interstitial disappears, the action
  "Cancel" is recorded, unless the Interstitial disappeared because of
  the "Open in Chrome Incognito" or "Open in Chrome" buttons.
- `IOS.IncognitoInterstitial.Settings` to record actions in the Privacy
  Settings, when the user enables the Incognito Interstitial switch or
  disables it.
