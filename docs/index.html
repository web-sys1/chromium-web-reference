Fix placement of additional data in NGPhysicalBoxFragment

Before this CL the computations were not considering alignment requirements or checking them, essentially meaning that alignment could break at any time when fields were added/removed.

For sheriffs: since now the computation of addresses of additional data
is slightly more expensive, this may lead to some perf regressions. If
this happens, we may consider static-asserting that alignment of some of
the additional data is enforced by the compiler (for both
no-pointer-compression and pointer-compression builds).
