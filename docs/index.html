field for:
- profile-wide variables for device-wide networks, such as
  DEVICE_SERIAL_NUMBER
- profile-wide variables for user-specific networks, such as LOGIN_EMAIL
- per-network variables based on a selected client certificate, such as
  CERT_SUBJECT_COMMON_NAME
