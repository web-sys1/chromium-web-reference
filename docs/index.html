Lock controls when bad capture and not ready.

The idea is that when the last capture is stale, and we're entering in
motion, we'd lock the browser controls. This has two goals, to ensure we
don't show the controls when the capture is stale, and to ensure we
don't take a capture during a motion event.

When this was originally written, it'd only lock when the controls were
ready for a capture, but this doesn't fully protect us from keeping a
stale capture from showing. So this CL relaxes the conditions of which
we lock the controls, hopefully to show bad captures less often.
