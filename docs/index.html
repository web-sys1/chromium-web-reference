[PA][BRP] Disable *DoubleFree tests when BRP is on

Upon the first free, an encoded free-list-next pointer is slapped at
the beginning of a slot span, which overrwites BRP's ref-count. This
may lead to (underteministically) bypassing the double-free check upon
the second free.
