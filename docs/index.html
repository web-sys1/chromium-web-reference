ash: Add hide animation to bubble launcher

The hide animation is a opacity fade-out and bounds shrink. Keep the
views::Widget alive until the end of the animation, then CloseNow().
This means the launcher is considered "showing" until the hide
animation completes, so attempts to Show() during the animation will
be blocked.

As an optimization, hide the scroll view gradient mask before the
animation. On kevin, official branded release build, this improves
smoothness from 47 to 65, P=0.01 for a difference. 65 is not great,
but the animation looks OK in practice. I think there might be a
single jank, and the animation is so short (100ms = 6 frames) that
any dropped frame really hurts the number.

Eliminate ScopedScrollViewGradientDisabler in favor of creating and
deleting the entire ScrollViewGradientHelper class. This simplifies
removing the gradient mask during hide. All the views that can
naturally own a Disabler are deleted at the end of the animation.
With this approach we don't have to worry about a Disabler going out
of scope and re-creating the mask layer.
