[cc] Fix flaky crashing browser_tests on ChromeOS

The crash happened when we committed from the main thread and the
high res tiling was dropped because its scale was out of the range
of min and max scales.

To avoid the crash, remove SanityCheckTilingState() from
CleanUpTilingsOnActiveLayer() to avoid the DCHECK failure at the
beginning of UpdateTiles(), and call SanityCheckTilingState() from
the original callsites after updating the tilings.
