arc: Simplify session cleanup after NearbyShare close reason fix

With the fix in https://crrev.com/c/3120239, we will route the
appropriate close reason for the Nearby Share bubble as either
success or cancel. Since we were only calling OnNearbyShareClosed()
previously with kCancel reason, OnCleanupSession() was not being called
in certain scenarios and we needed an extra session_finished_callback_
as WA in OnNearbyShareClosed(). Now this is not needed and we also want
to avoid calling the callback twice in OnNearbyShareClosed().
