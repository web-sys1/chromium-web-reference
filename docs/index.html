Remove duplicated observer logic from `TouchToFillKeyboardSuppressor`

`TouchToFillKeyboardSuppressor` had 2 observer events with the exact
same logic.

These 2 observer events are:
`ContentAutofillDriverFactory::Observer::OnContentAutofillDriverStateChanged`
`AutofillManager::Observer::OnAutofillManagerStateChanged`

Both of this methods are directly/indirectly called from:
`AutofillDriverFactory::SetLifecycleStateAndNotifyObservers`
(https://source.chromium.org/chromium/chromium/src/+/main:components/autofill/core/browser/autofill_driver_factory.cc;l=15;drc=d94b74f8f2dc4344fe7149c797d096b0882c6a1b)
, so in most cases they were both called one right after the other.

This could have led to crashes, because both of them attempted to remove
the same observation.

Why no crashes occurred so far and why the previous design was still
fragile can be found in this doc:
http://docs/document/d/1pu4mSl1q7VmlbGpZJUUz0o27EW9tquRXA6gqABO1lmw
