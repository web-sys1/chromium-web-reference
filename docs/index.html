[Lacros] Flip can_release_capture_ flag for Lacros tab dragging

Please refer to this document (go/tab-drag-release-capture) for full manual test details and a close look at how `DraggingState` is modified along each step.

`can_release_capture_` is a flag inside `tab_drag_controller.cc` that dictates, at the moment when a tab/multiple tabs is dragged outside of a browser window, what the next DraggingState will become. The next DraggingState and its enclosing move loop (a special runloop that handles tab drag events outside of browser area) will further influence the subsequent tab dragging behavior.

It is suspected that the current flag value, which is false, may cause edge cases such as the one discussed in http://go/crrv/c/4202947. By flipping this value to true for Lacros, we may be able to solve that issue and other relevant tab dragging bugs.
