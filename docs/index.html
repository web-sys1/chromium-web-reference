base/android: Revive ThreadInstructionCount::Now()

Convenience wrappers around __NR_perf_event_open. They were recently
removed as part of cleanup in tracing [1].

Bring it back as a standalone utility, only for Android. It allows to
roughly compare performance of two functions that are being called
frequently. Recent example of such estimation: [2].
