infra: Bump build timeout of focal FYI bots to 16 hours

The test pool is so small that the CI builds can take quite a while, eg:
https://ci.chromium.org/ui/p/chromium/builders/ci/Linux%20ChromiumOS%20MSan%20Focal/51/overview

That had a 10 hour timeout, and the build just managed to finish in
time. So to avoid occasional timeouts, this bumps the ceiling to 16
hours on both CI and trybots.
