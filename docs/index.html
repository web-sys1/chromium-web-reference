media/gpu/vaapi/test: Don't destroy VA Context in non-keyframe

vaDestroyContext() cleans up internal states of a VA-API driver and
makes the driver unable to reference earlier frames. Therefore, a VA
context must not be destroyed unless the succeeding frames don't
reference the earlier frames, i.e. the frame is keyframe.
