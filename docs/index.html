Original change's description:
> [AW-IPP] Add IPP metrics based on eligible traffic
>
> Currently, there exists IPP (IP Protection) metrics that
> measure the amount of traffic sent to and received from the proxies.
> This is measured based on all parameters relevant to IPP being
> applicable (e.g, tokens being retrievable, token cache not
> being empty, etc).
>
> In WebView, we want to make initial measurements even before
> the token logic is set up. This is vital to estimate the traffic
> based on different candidate policies. This CL adds metrics to
> measure the traffic sent to and received from proxies
> based on the eligibility of traffic (match based on the MDL).
>
