[MFVEA] Resolve the corner case of VP9 consecutive keyframes issue

Previously, to avoid corruption caused by consecutive keyframes,
a fake frame was inserted between two keyframes and then discarded
after encoding. This approach handles most cases, but there are
some corner cases where the inserted fake frame ends up being
encoded as a keyframe.

This CL:
  1. Encode an additional fake frame to fix the corner case.
  2. Introduce |effective_options| and remove the redundant
     |force_key_frame| calculation in ProcessInput() that was
     duplicating logic already handled in the Encode().
