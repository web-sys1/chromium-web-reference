[AW] Remove ResourceGetIdentifier2 histogram

Mark ResourceGetIdentifier2 as obsolete and remove all the record
statements from AndroidProtocolHandler. Histograms records showed
that reflection succeeds to get resource ids in 1.7% of the cases.
Despite being a small percentage, removing reflection would break
some of the apps. So will keep reflection as a fail back.
