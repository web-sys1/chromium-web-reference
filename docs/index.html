Tick keyboard and scripted smooth scrolls on the compositor thread.

After unification, we can update the scroll offset on the compositor
thread, even if the scroller is not composited or otherwise repaints on
main.  ScrollableArea::ShouldScrollOnMainThread, which was only used by
the scroll animator classes, is modified and renamed to achieve this for
smooth scrolls that are initiated from main.

WebPagePopup is still a special case that needs to tick on main (see
crbug.com/1503709 and crbug.com/1270173).
