viz: Enable SkiaRenderer unconditionally on CrOS

SkiaRenderer is currently disabled on a small set of CrOS boards
(MT8173, RK3399) that use the legacy video decoder. The legacy video
decoder wraps video buffers in legacy mailboxes instead of
SharedImages which SkiaRenderer cannot handle when processing for
overlays.

Now that SkiaRenderer is fully launched on the vast majority of CrOS
devices, it's dangerous to leave these old devices on GLRenderer as
GLRenderer no longer receives updates and lacks any automated test
coverage (not to mention the performance benefits of SkiaRenderer).
Furthermore, MT8173 and RK3399 have limited support for overlays in
the first place.

Thus, this CL makes the tradeoff between SkiaRenderer and overlays by
disabling overlays on legacy video decoder boards and enabling
SkiaRenderer unconditionally. It also moves the Disallow Direct
Video Decoder flag to ui/ozone/. The flag was moved to viz purely for
visibility [1], but now it is no longer needed there.

[1] https://chromium-review.googlesource.com/c/chromium/src/+/2518460
