sharing: move logging for Sharing.AnyShareStarted

This was being logged at the //chrome layer, but that makes it very
awkward to log shares initiated in //components/browser_ui,
especially those that happen via direct calls to ShareHelper. This
change moves the logging of those events into ShareHelper, which
causes ShareHelper#shareDirectly to now be logged correctly.
