Avoid dangling pointers in global shortcut bindings

BoundCommand used a raw_ptr<Observer> to
ExtensionCommandsGlobalRegistry. After extension unload, stale entries
in bound_commands_ could outlive the registry and trigger
dangling-pointer detection at shutdown.

Switch to a WeakPtr-bound base::RepeatingCallback and add
GlobalAcceleratorListener::PruneStaleCommands() to explicitly remove
stale callbacks during teardown.

Also update Linux OnCommandsChanged() to drop stale prefix entries and
close the XDG portal session when no global shortcuts remain.
