[CrashFix] Fix null pointer exception in AccountManagementFragment.canAddAccounts

AccountManagementFragment.updateAccountsList() is called by
AccountManagerFacadeProvider when accounts are fetched.

AccountManagementFragment.updateAccountsList() is responsible for
updating preferences depending on account fetch. The crash is due to
this method being called while the fragment is detached from the
activity.

One way to solve this crash would have been to remove the callback from
AccountManagerFacadeProvider when AccountManagementFragment is detached
from activity. But currently that support is not provided by
AccountManagerFacadeProvider.

This cl instead checks whether the fragment is attached to the activity
at the beginning of updateAccountsList() method.
