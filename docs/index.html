Remove Android Screen Capture Infobar

Clank infobars are being deprecated in favor of the new messages API.
However, this particular infobar is not actually shipped (all behavior
is behind a flag) and we are not sure what the final 'permission' flow
for this feature will be. (Though it is likely we may need to devise
or use some form of picker rather than the simple prompt we have now).

Rather than continue to block the messages team on their migration or
do potentially throw-away work for the screen capture permission flow,
this change simply removes the infobar for now.
