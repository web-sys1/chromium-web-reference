Record emoji glyph run success/brokenness

FontMatchingMetrics thankfully already implements the logic for
recording font related statistics for the lifetime of a Document or
WorkerGlobalContext. Borrow this infrastructure for tracking during
shaping what the percentage of successfully shaped to unsuccessfully
shape emoji clusters is, based on the assumption that each emoji and
emoji sequence should show as one non-tofu glyph.

Needed for a limited time for tracking whether switching to retrieving
the emoji font "Noto Color Emoji" from GMSCore (see issue 1142444) moves
the needle and can increase the number of successfully shaped emoji and
lead to better glyph coverage.
