Amend usages of non-exported receivers

Receivers which were previously set up to listen for a mixture of
protected broadcasts and non-protected self-targetting broadcasts have
been split up into separate receivers, registered via the appropriate
registerProtectedBroadcastReceiver or
registerNonExportedBroadcastReceiver wrapper methods.

For non-exported receivers, IntentUtils's addTrustedIntentExtras and
isTrustedIntentFromSelf have added to emulate the behaviour of
non-exported receivers on pre-T devices. (Note that the more
mainstream approach outside of chromium of using permissions cannot be
used for WebView because that runs with the embedding app's
permissions.)

setPackage() has also been added to avoid Intents being sent to other
applications, including other instances of Chrome or WebView.
