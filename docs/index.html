[Autofill Assistant] Move onboarding into its own package.

The onboarding has become complex enough to split it off into its own
package. With this change, the two implementations (bottom sheet and
popup dialog) will become implementation-details and are no longer
available directly in other packages. Rather, callers should use
the onboarding factory to create onboarding instances.

This CL also fixes a crash in debug builds with split modules by
changing the visibility of methods in the preferences util.
