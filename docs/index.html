Move ownership of DataProtectionNavgationObserver from navigation handle to DataProtectionNavigationController

This refactor does not affect the behavior of enterprise watermarking.
In order to implement single page app support, the navigation observer
must persist after the navigation ends, since verdicts may be received
later than that. A future CL will destroy the navigation observer after
the later of two events: `DidFinishNavigation()` and
`OnRealtimeLookupComplete()`.
