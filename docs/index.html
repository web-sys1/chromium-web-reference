[Profiles] Take a ScopedProfileKeepAlive during profile deletion

This CL is part of the refactoring of profile deletion, following the
refactoring of ephemeral profiles.

We take a ProfileDeletionProcess ScopedProfileKeepAlive to avoid the
profile from being randomly unloaded and loaded again. It is created in MaybeScheduleProfileForDeletion() and passed to the success callback of
CloseAllBrowsersWithProfile(). The keep alive is released after
OnLoadProfileForProfileDeletion().
