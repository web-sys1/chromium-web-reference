> [Actor] Move HandoffButtonController ownership to ActorUiContentsContainerController
>
> Refactors the ownership of HandoffButtonController from
> ActorUiTabController to ActorUiContentsContainerController. This change

> - HandoffButtonController registers itself with ActorUiTabController.

> - Removed ActorUiTabControllerFactory::CreateHandoffButtonController.
> - Updated unit tests to reflect ownership and registration changes.
