[TTFCC] Fix that keyboard accessory suggestions are not shown after BS
dismissal

The problem is that the KA doesn't show the credit card suggestions
after the user dismissed the bottom sheet.
This happens because `touch_to_fill_delegate_->IsShowingTouchToFill()`
stays true after bottom sheet dismissal without selection any action.
Then here
https://source.chromium.org/chromium/chromium/src/+/main:components/autofill/core/browser/browser_autofill_manager.cc;l=1206?q=browser_autofill&ss=chromium%2Fchromium%2Fsrc
the autofill manager thinks that the bottom sheet is being shown and
there is no need in KA suggestions.

The solution: introduce the `OnDismissed` method into
`TouchToFillDelegate` and call it from `TouchToFillCreditCardController`
to notify the delegate that the BS is not being displayed any more.
