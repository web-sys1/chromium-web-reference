[WebLayer] Encapsulate PaymentRequestUi in PaymentUiService

PaymentRequestUi is the details of PaymentUiService and so CPRService
should not have access to it. This CL hides PaymentRequestUi from
CPRService. After the CL, CPRService uses it by the proxy of
PaymentUiService.

For the methods that have been moved into PaymentUiService, this CL
adds the null-check in them.
