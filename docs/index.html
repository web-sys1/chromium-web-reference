> Original change's description:
> > [HitTestOpaqueness] Record hit test data for scrolling backgrounds
> >
> > This doesn't help much to HitTestOpaqueness itself because the visible
> > area of scrolling contents is also covered by the ScrollHitTestData of
> > the scroller which ensures opaqueness to hit test if the scroller is
> > opaque to hit test.
> >
> > The actual benefits are:
> > - This may improve scroll performance for scrolling contents that
> >   don't have a background. The new hit test data will make most
> >   scrolling contents layers to be always the same size as the
> >   scroll overflow regardless of the cull rect (which may change on
> >   scroll), to avoid full layer rerasterization when the cull rect
> >   change affects the bounds of the painted scrolling contents.
> > - This may help crbug.com/324285520 because the new hit test data
> >   carries special hit test data.
> > - This also prepares for a CL for UseRecordedBoundsForTiling to
> >   prevent extra rasterization (caused by change of the origin of the
> >   tiling rect) during scroll.
