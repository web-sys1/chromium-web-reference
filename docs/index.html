Inline AwContents AwViewMethods.

This gets rid of the AwContents methods that just call through to
mAwViewMethodsImpl. Doing this groups the methods that are to do with
View responsibilities and prevents people from adding logic to the
AwContents method instead of the AwViewMethodsImpl method, which is
likely a bug with full screen.
