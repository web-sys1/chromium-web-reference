Re-write FROM_HISTORY intents to always be MAIN intents.

When an Activity self-finishes without removing its task, and is then
launched from the recents/overview menu, it's launched with its base
intent, which could be a View intent if that's what initially launched
the Activity. This could lead to unexpectedly launching URLs extra
times, firing app redirects multiple times, etc.

To avoid this, we can just re-write incoming FROM_HISTORY intents to
look like the intents Android sends us when we don't self-finish and
are resumed from history after being killed (MAIN Intents with the
FROM_HISTORY flag).
