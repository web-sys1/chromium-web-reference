[gpu_image_decode_cache/metrics] Re-enable metrics for cache.

This CL re-enables some expired "Renderer4.*" metrics relating to
the GPU Image Decode Cache, which keep track of how often images in the
cache are used.

The owners are these metrics are also updated to remove those who have left Google and to include thiabaud@ and vmpstr@ as owners.
