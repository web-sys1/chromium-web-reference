Add use-counters for |OffsetWidth| etc. when block-in-inline

This patch adds use counters for:
1. LayoutInline::OffsetWidth
2. LayoutInline::OffsetHeight
3. LayoutInline::PhysicalLinesBoundingBox
when they have continuations. They are ignored in legacy and
NG, but NGBlockInInline includes them as of ToT. The use
counters are to estimate the impact of the behavior change.

1 and 2 are called from:
a. HTMLElement::Offset{Width,Height}ForBinding
b. Element::Offset{Width,Height} (internal version of a., used
   by inspector and modules/xr.)
c. ComputedStyleUtils::ValueForPositionOffset
d. MediaControlSliderElement::TrackWidth

3 is their underlying function, used by some more callers:
e. LayoutBoxModelObject::UpdateStickyPositionConstraints
f. LayoutBox::ContainingBlockLogicalHeightForPositioned
g. PaintLayer::UpdateSize
h. CSSMaskPainter::MaskBoundingBox
i. InspectorHighlightBase::BuildNodeQuads
j. HasRenderedNonAnonymousDescendantsWithHeight (visible_units.cc)
https://source.chromium.org/search?q=PhysicalLinesBoundingBox%20-file:test.cc&sq=&ss=chromium%2Fchromium%2Fsrc

CSSWG issue:
https://github.com/w3c/csswg-drafts/issues/6588
