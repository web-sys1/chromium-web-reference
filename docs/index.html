Add registerProtectedBroadcastReceiver and presubmit check for registerReceiver

Adds a registerProtectedBroadcastReceiver wrapper function, similar to
the existing exported and non-exported variants. Receivers for protected
broadcasts should not use either RECIEVER_EXPORTED or
RECEIVER_NOT_EXPORTED in the internal call to registerReceiver.

Adds a presubmit check to prevent calls to unwrapped registerReceiver
being added in future CLs. We will be auditing and replacing
registerReceiver and wrapper call sites in subsequent CLs.
