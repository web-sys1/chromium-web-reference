Fix flaky test BackForwardCacheBrowserTest.VideoSuspendAndResume.

On very slow devices, the time between suspend and resume can still be
very long. Particularly since the post-cache measurement is done by
executing JS from the browser, so requires an IPC.

The fix here is to:
- use pageshow and pagehide to measure the interval. These are the 2 JS measurement points that are closest together and require no IPCs.
- play the video at .1x speed to make it a 10s video as on Android it sometimes plays fully before the page even enters BFCache
