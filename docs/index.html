VideoTrackAdapter: avoid odd-placed visible_rect().

Video frames getting cropped inside the VideoTrackAdapter are
interpreted with a byteswapped UV plane from some consumers
when the output frame's visible_rect()'s x() coordinate is odd
and the source format is NV12.

Fix this by rounding down the x coordinate to an even value.

This CL additionally gets rid of GMB/Texture-only logic related to
rounding width and height upwards to even, and also handles the less
visible problem appearing when the y() coordinate is odd (it just shifts
colors half a pixel.
