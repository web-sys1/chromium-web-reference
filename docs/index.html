[base] Prepare //chrome/installer/util/shell_util for string16 switch

This change prepares //chrome/installer/util/shell_util for the upcoming
base::string16 switch on Windows. It performs the following mechanical
changes:

* Replace base::string16 with std::wstring
* Replace base::char16 with wchar_t
* Replace base::StringPiece16 with base::WStringPiece
* Replace #include "base/strings/string16.h" with #include <string>
* Replace STRING16_LITERAL("...") with L"..."
* Replace UTF16 conversion functions with their wide equivalents
