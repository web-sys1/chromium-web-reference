Add more comprehensive metrics for download deep scanning

This CL adds a new metric, SBClientDownload.DeepScanEvent, which tracks
several events that were previously only recorded implicitly in other
metrics (such as prompts shown being equal to
SBClientDownload.CheckDownloadStats's ADVANCED_PROTECTION_PROMPT
bucket). This metric will be used to compare the effectiveness of the
old and new UX.

This CL also removes references to Advanced Protection due to the
previously launched expansion of deep scanning to ESB users.
