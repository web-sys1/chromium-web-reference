[Save PDF to Drive] AccountChooserController listens to primary account changes.

According to yaqs/533072923959754752,
GetExtendedAccountInfoForAccountsWithRefreshToken can return a primary
account without a valid refresh token. To ensure only accounts with
valid refresh tokens are shown, AccountChooserController (1) observes
OnErrorStateOfRefreshTokenUpdatedForAccount and OnPrimaryAccountChanged
for primary account changes, and (2) GetProfileInfo explicitly checks
the primary account for a valid refresh token. GetProfileInfo also
ensures the accounts are populated with critical account information,
since the OnErrorStateOfRefreshTokenUpdatedForAccount and
OnPrimaryAccountChanged do not guarantee extended account info.
