[RQ] Records histograms for extraction of the repeatable queries

Adds histograms for extraction of the most repeated search terms from
the in-memory URLDatabase. These histograms are similar to those of the
local history (zero-)prefix suggestions. They record the duration it
takes to extract and sort the keyword search terms as well as the number
of extracted search terms.
