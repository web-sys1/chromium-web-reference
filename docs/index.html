[PhoneHub] Modify keepalive intervals for battery performance

This CL increases the Nearby Connections layer keepalive from
15 seconds to 25 seconds, the WebRTC keepalive from 2.5 seconds
to 25 seconds, and both timeouts to 10 minutes to account for
when the phone goes into deep sleep mode, during which it can
take up to 8 minutes to receive keepalives from the phone.

The Nearby Connections keepalive values gets negotiated by the client
that requests the connection so they will be used by the Phone as well.

The WebRTC keepalive will be changed on the phone side by setting the
flags
mediums_web_rtc_ice_check_interval_strong_connectivity_ms = 15000
mediums_web_rtc_stable_writable_connection_ping_interval_ms = 30000
mediums_web_rtc_ice_connection_receiving_timeout_ms = 600000

See go/phone-hub-battery-fix for more information.

Gated by nearby-keep-alive-fix flag.
