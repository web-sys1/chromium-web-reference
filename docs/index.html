Revert "Don't track sticky viewport constrained objects"

This reverts commit fb76828e40e9013b5f7d53fa592b1b418c4725b1.

Reason for revert: Will land after we finish the
OptimizeViewportConstrainedPaintInvalidation experiment.

Original change's description:
> Don't track sticky viewport constrained objects
>
> Sticky-position objects are tracked by their containing scrollers
> (including the viewport scroller [1]). There is no need to track
> sticky viewport constrained objects in the LocalFrameView.
>
> As now the term "viewport constrained object" is just an alias of
> "fixed-position object", and it has been used to indicate different
> things in our code, to avoid confusion, rename
> ViewportConstrainedObject to FixedPositionObject.
>
> [1] I believe tracking of sticky viewport constrained objects was
>     needed before RootLayerScrolls when the layout viewport scrolling
>     was handled in LocalFrameView instead of the LayoutView mostly as
>     a normal LayoutBox.
>
