[GPUChannel] Mark accessor as test-only

GPUChannel exposes ImageDecodeAcceleratorStub via an accessor. On
inspection, this accessor is used only by tests. Mark it as ForTesting
to ensure that this stays the case.

In addition to just being good practice, this helps concretely as
ImageDecodeAcceleratorStub heavily uses GLImage, which we are trying
to eliminate, and thus locking down its accessibility is a good thing.
