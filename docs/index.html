[hid] Add a blocklist for HID device functionality

This CL adds a blocklist to block device functionality that should
not be accessible from javascript. The blocklist may be extended
by setting the WebHIDBlocklist.blocklist_additions Finch variation
parameter.

The blocklist may be bypassed by passing
allow_protected_reports=true when connecting to the device.
