webnn: Clamp and shift negative indices to positive

The indices in gather can exceed the boundary of the `axis` dimension of
input, so add a clamp operation to make sure the value in range of `-N`
(inclusive) to `N` (exclusive), where `N = input.dimensions[axis]`, and
shift negative indices to positive with `where(lesser(indices,
constant(0)), indices, add(indices, constant(input.dimensions[axis])))`.
