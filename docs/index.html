Add flag to override variations seed fetch interval time

This CL:

1. Adds a `--variations-seed-fetch-interval` switch that overrides the
seed fetch interval time (the default time is 30 minutes). The minimum
interval specifiable through the switch is 1 minute.

2. Adds a `--disable-variations-seed-fetch-throttling` switch that
disables seed fetch throttling on mobile platforms. Hence, the seed
will be fetched every time the app enters the foreground, and on
startup.
