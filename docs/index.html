[PaymentRequest] Remove invalid DCHECK in PaymentHandlerWebFlowViewController

This DCHECK was checking that the page loaded in the PaymentHandler
web_contents had the Payments permission policy set. However that policy is
required to *call* PaymentRequest, not to be loaded by it! As written, this
DCHECK would trigger on basically every web PaymentHandler.
