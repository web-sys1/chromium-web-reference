AW UMA: split kAllWebViewsHandled into two cases

This changes the Android.WebView.OnRenderProcessGoneResult histogram to
split kAllWebViewsHandled into two cases, depending on whether the
renderer death was because of a "crash" or a "kill" (OOM/update). We
currently make the distinction if the renderer death is not handled by
the app, however we didn't make a distinction when the death is handled
by the app.

This extra info should help investigate a strange result we saw in the
data (see https://crbug.com/1272940#c1).
