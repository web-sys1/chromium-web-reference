[ios] Set Appbar tint adjustment mode to normal

Setting the tintAdjustmentMode to UIViewTintAdjustmentModeNormal on
the stack view ensures that its subviews retain their original tint
color even when the view's window is dimmed (e.g., when a modal or
popover is presented). This prevents the app bar elements from
appearing grayed out or inactive in those states.
