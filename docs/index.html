Componentize chrome/browser/ui/views/send_tab_to_self/

This CL extracts the send_tab_to_self views files from the monolithic
chrome/browser/ui:ui target into their own BUILD.gn with dedicated
targets:

 - :send_tab_to_self (public headers)
 - :impl (implementation)

Internal-only headers (manage_account_devices_link_view.h and
send_tab_to_self_bubble_device_button.h) are kept in :impl to avoid
a circular dependency from the headers target back to :ui.
