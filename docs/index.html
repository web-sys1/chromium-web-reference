Move ImportMap responsibilities from ModulatorImplBase to Modulator

Before this CL, `Modulator::RegisterImportMap()` was also responsible
for throwing the V8 error. This CL moves it to
`PendingImportMap::RegisterImportMap()`.

The above makes `Modulator::RegisterImportMap()` trivial enough that it
can be migrated to Modulator. This CL moves ImportMap management methods
from ModulatorImplBase to Modulator.

`Modulator::GetImportMap()` protected method is temporarily introduced,
so that `ModulatorImplBase::ResolveModuleSpecifier()` can make use of
`Modulator::import_map_`, until `ResolveModuleSpecifier()` is also
moved to Modulator.
