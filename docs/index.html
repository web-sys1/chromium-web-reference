[ios] Fix white spaces in chrome:// pages by updating the way to generate a snapshot

Previously, a snapshot for chrome:// pages was generated in the method
`-snapshotBaseView:frameInBaseView:`, which converts an UIView to an
UIImage with the size of `frameInBaseView`.
However, the method `-imageWithActions:` in UIGraphicsImageRenderer
and `-drawViewHierarchyInRect:` are buggy. They can't crop the image
when the size of a base image is larger than `frameInBaseView`.

Now, this CL divides `-snapshotBaseView:frameInBaseView:` method into
2 steps; 1) converts an UIView to an UIImage 2) crops an UIImage by
`frameInBaseView`.

This CL affects chrome:// pages and new tab pages.
