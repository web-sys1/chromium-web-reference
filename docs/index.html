Add nullptr check and reduce naming confusion

invalidation_service_ refers to two different variables in
   AffiliatedInvalidationServiceProviderImpl and
   AffiliatedInvalidationServiceProviderImpl::
       InvalidationServiceObserver
This has led to confusion in analyzing crbug.com/1178064

This CL renames one of the two variables to avoid this confusion.
It also moves the DCHECK(invalidation_service_) to the constructor
of the Observer, where it is actually required since the pointer does
not change after construction.
