libstdc++: do not use operator<< in std::unique_ptr in XR CameraIndicationServer

Building with libstdc++ fails in XR CameraIndicationServer, trying to
use operator<< on an std::unique_ptr. This operator is defined for
C++20 standard, but not for C++17 (which we use) or C++14.
