media/gpu, ChromeOS: remove YV12 from kPreferredRenderableFourccs

kPreferredRenderableFourccs is used by VideoDecoderPipeline to decide
if the output of the platform video decoder (VideoDecoderMixin) needs
an ImageProcessor or not.

In practice we only need two formats now: NV12 and P010. YV12 is here
possibly as a better-safe-than, but in practice no platform produces
YV12 and not NV12, so let's remove it for simplicity.

(rk3399 and qc7180: NV12, mtk8173 MT21, mtk8183,8192,8195: MM21,
Intel/AMD also support NV12).

No new functionality intended.
