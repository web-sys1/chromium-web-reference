[Vertical Tabs] Detect when Touch Exits Tabstrip

This change implements a way for the touch helper to determine when the
cursor or touch drag has departed from the side panel. This is critical
functionality for determining when to create a shadow for dragging a tab
or tab group out of VT and into a new window.

Testing is included.
