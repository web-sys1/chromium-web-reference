Fix negative GetPreferredRenderInterval() for MediaStreams.

WebMediaPlayerMSCompositor::RenderWithoutAlgorithm() is receiving
frames with timestamps in the past which leads to a negative value
for |last_render_length_| and GetPreferredRenderInterval().

The cause appears to be due to camera devices resetting their
timestamps when started and stopped outside of the renderer. It's
unclear to me if this is also a bug that should be fixed, since it
seems likely to lead to a/v sync issues.
