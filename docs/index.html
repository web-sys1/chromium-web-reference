Remove inactive OWNERS from Blink

This is an attempt to remove inactive OWNERS from Blink. In 2020 Summer,
I wrote a tool to list potentialy inactive OWNERS in the past three
months. I added the list to the spreadsheet
(https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1-YyuFQY3M6W6KEdwmiylLX7GpOIGMgIs1Jh4S7qQ820/edit#gid=388628519)
and asked the listed people to flip "Drop me from the list" to
"Keep me in the list" if they want to stay as an OWNER. I sent the
reminder email three times. This CL removes OWNERS who are still marked
as "Drop me from the list".

Note 1: Before landing CL, I will send another email to all the OWNERS
being removed from this CL.

Note 2: It's possible that you couldn't respond to my emails because you
were on vacation etc. In that case, please simply add your name back to
OWNERS. haraken@ will LGTM the change.
