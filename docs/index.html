Make the entire Handoff button clickable.

Previously, the Handoff button applied its content padding within the
custom FrameView. This caused the actual LabelButton to be smaller than
its visual appearance, resulting in the button edges being unclickable.

This change moves the content padding directly into the
HandoffLabelButton by applying a padded border. The FrameView now only
accounts for the shadow and background insets.
