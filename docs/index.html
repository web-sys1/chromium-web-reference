Original change's description:
> Android: Normalize D8 desugared lambda names
>
> D8 has various formats for its desugared lambdas. The new tests in
> apkanalyzer_test.py show the various ways D8 is desugaring lambdas (and
> some of the ways it has desugared lambdas in the past). It is important
> that our lambda normalization algorithm is backward compatible since ToT
> supersize may run on older codebases where we use an older version of
> D8/R8.
>
> The idea remains the same, normalize D8's lambda names so that they are
> easily compared with other lambdas generated by Bazel/classic desugar or
> older versions of D8/R8. This means we replace most of the name with a
> lambda prefix, suffixed with the number for that particular class. One
> special case is that of outlined (or External, as D8 names them)
> lambdas. These lambdas have a special prefix to denote that they may be
> used by more than just the class in their name (and they reside in the
> top level APK directory in terms of their object path).
>
> Removed duplication in the lambda normalization tests since there are
> various ways that D8 desugars lambdas according to location and library.
>
