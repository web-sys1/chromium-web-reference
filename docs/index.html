[Omnibox] update both icon and its bg when icon's visibility is updated

The CL https://crrev.com/c/3976177 marks the icon's background is GONE
when the icon is GONE, but only set the background back to INVISIBLE
when the ravamp 2 experiment is on and focus change[1].
To make the icon and the icon's background always update at same
time, we always update their parents instead.
