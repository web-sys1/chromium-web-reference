Revert "desk_profiles: Do not restore to the previous desk for new browser"

This reverts commit 1925bdc0c1bf8cd6ecd595a30333cbc946cbbadf.

Reason for revert: The change breaks the virtual desk aspect of "continue where you left off" in that all browser windows are restored to the current desk rather than whatever desk they were on. For now we will revert this change.

Original change's description:
> desk_profiles: Do not restore to the previous desk for new browser
>
> With Desk Profiles feature, chromeos can now associate a lacros profile
> with a virtual desk. When launching a new browser within the desk, a
> browser for that profile will be launched. With session restore, it
> restores the new browser window to its previous desk.
>
> This CL proposes that when lacros and Desk Profiles are both enabled,
> the new browser window should *not* be restored to its previous desk.
>
> Quick demo can be found at - b/319730718.
>
